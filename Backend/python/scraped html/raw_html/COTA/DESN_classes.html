Reserve Capacity - Reserved seats (AKA reserve capacity) is a new functionality added to the registration system that can save a certain number of seats for a specific group of students.
Zero Cost Course Materials - California Community Colleges and California State Universities now include a no cost symbol in the online campus course schedule that exclusively use digital course materials that are free of charge to students and therefore not required to be purchased.

Prerequisite and/or co-requisite - see University Catalog.

Double numbered course. 400 level/undergraduates; 500 level/graduates.

For specific majors only - inquire department office.

17

Admission preference given to majors.

21

Open to upper division students only.

305

Online Asynchronous Instruction: instructor and students participate at different times; no set schedule for face-to-face or synchronous online meetings; check course syllabus for details.

306

Online Synchronous Instruction: instructor and students normally participate at the same time on a set schedule, students expected to be available to participate on indicated days at specified times; may include asynchronous online work; check course syllabus for details. Camera use may be required for synchronous online courses. Students in need of appropriate technology resources should contact ATS-help@csulb.edu

307

Hybrid Instruction: Class consists of both face-to-face and asynchronous (no meet times) online class meetings. Check course syllabus for details.

309

Online Mixed Meeting (Synchronous and Asynchronous) Instruction: Instructor and students participate online at the same time on a set schedule, and online at different times. Check course syllabus for details. Camera use may be required for synchronous online courses. Student in need of appropriate technology resources should contact ATS-help@csulb.edu

310

311
